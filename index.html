Our Story

What an Exceptional Avenue to Care

Heritage Health Services is a personal care agency proudly serving the people of Racine County, Kenosha County, Milwaukee County, Dane County, Dodge County, Jefferson County, Ozaukee County, Rock County, Walworth County, Washington County and Waukesha County in Wisconsin. We work with Medicaid HMOs. We are currently accepting new private pay Clients as well as those individuals who receive Title 19, BadgerCare, or Medicaid.

Every day thousands of Wisconsin residents are called upon to be the caregiver for their loved one with disabilities. As the caregiver, they may have to take time off from their jobs, cut back on their hours, or in some cases, are forced to go so far as to quit working outside of the home all together. These are the choices that thousands of Wisconsin residents face everyday in order to take care of a family member or close friend who is no longer able to do so themselves.

What if you, as a Wisconsin resident, had the ability to choose who took care of you in your own home? What if a caregiver had the ability to get paid for the care that you're already giving to your loved one in need? Well, you do!

Not only can caregivers get paid for taking care of a loved one, select Wisconsin residents have the right to choose their own personal caregiver! Individuals who receive Title 19 or Medicaid have the right to choose their own personal care aid.

Not only are caregivers struggling with the knowledge and stress that their loved ones need help, they are also facing their own personal and financial needs. What many Wisconsin residents don't realize is that there is a huge light at the end of this tunnel of despair and worry.

Heritage Health Services is here to help the residents of Milwaukee, Racine, Kenosha and all of Southeast Wisconsin to hire the caregiver they choose and in turn, giving them the peace of mind of no strangers in their home. This is a sure way for Wisconsin residents to know they are getting the best care possible by someone they know and trust.

No longer will you be faced with the fear of strangers in your own home while worrying how your loved ones will make ends meet. We offer FREE training for your loved ones, allowing them to get paid for providing the care you need in the privacy of your own home. Whether you choose friends or family as your personal care worker, or you decide to go with one of our qualified and experienced professionals, we are here to assist you every step of the way through your short or long term care needs.

Contact Heritage Health Services today to request a free in-home Registered Nurse assessment and start the process of receiving the care you deserve!

Personal Care Services

Comprehensive In-Home Care

A Registered Nurse-led support team will work side by side with you to help tailor and develop your very own customized personal plan of care to meet all of your specific care needs.

01

Activities of Daily Living (ADLs)

From grooming and dressing to nutritious meal preparation, we ensure your daily routine is handled with dignity. Our goal is to maintain your independence while providing the support needed to start and end your day safely.

02

Safe Transfers & Mobility

Falls are the leading cause of injury in the home. Our caregivers are trained in safe transfer techniques, using gait belts and assistive devices to help you move from bed to chair or room to room without risk.

03

Adaptive Dressing Support

We help you stay comfortable and confident. Whether it's managing compression stockings or choosing appropriate seasonal attire, our assistants provide the extra hands needed for a stress-free dressing routine.

04

Hygiene & Skin Integrity

Professional hygiene care is about more than looking good—it's about health. We provide hair, nail, and skin care that prevents irritation and infections, keeping you refreshed and physically sound.

05

Respectful Incontinence Care

We prioritize your privacy and skin health. Our caregivers provide sensitive, professional assistance with toileting and catheter care, following strict clinical protocols to prevent skin breakdown and maintain comfort.

06

Bathing & Safety Oversight

The bathroom is the most hazardous room in the home. We provide the physical support and oversight needed for safe bathing and showering, turning a risky task into a relaxing, dignified experience.
